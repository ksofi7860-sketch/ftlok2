Our Beginning

1996

FineTech Engineering Corporation was founded with a vision to revolutionize the industrial fluid control industry through uncompromising quality and innovation.

His entrepreneurial spirit and strategic foresight have propelled us beyond geographical boundaries, establishing a distinguished presence in international markets.
