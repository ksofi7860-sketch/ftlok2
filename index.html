About Us

Established in 1990, FT-LOK is a trusted global manufacturer of high-quality instrumentation fittings and valves. With advanced manufacturing systems, strong R&D capabilities, and a commitment to engineering excellence, we consistently deliver products that meet international standards of performance, precision, and reliability.
Our growth is driven by an experienced team and forward-thinking leadership that ensure we stay aligned with evolving industry requirements.
At FT-LOK, we prioritize customer satisfaction, uphold the highest professional standards, and focus on building long-term, sustainable relationships with our employees, customers, suppliers, shareholders, and the broader community.
